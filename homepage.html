Instructions

Tic-tac-toe or Xs and Os is a paper-and-pencil game for two players, X and O, who take turns marking the spaces in a 3Ã—3 grid. The player who succeeds in placing three of their marks in a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal row is the winner.

Rules

  • If the player's guess matches the secret number, they win. The program should display the total number of guesses that were made.
  • If the player's guess contains a digit that matches one of the digits in the secret number and is in the same position, that is considered one "bull".
  • If the player's guess contains a digit that matches one of the digits in the secret number but is not in the same position, that is considered one "cow".
